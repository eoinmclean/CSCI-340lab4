Bug & Bovine Academy

A college where curiosity crawls, buzzes and moos

Welcome

Bug & Bovine Academy is a tiny campus built around fieldwork and observation. Students learn how groups coordinagte, how routines shape behaviour, and how ecosystems respond to care.

Departments

  • Insect Studies: Pollination, navigation, micro-engineering
  • School of Herd Thinking: communication, routine, social learning
  • Cattle and Grassland Systems: grazing, soil, sustainable farming

Campus life

Days start early, dew on the grass, a lecture under the fence line, lab groups measuring insect traffic.

Popular activites

  1. Buzz & Browse reading hour
  2. Microscopy pop-ups
  3. Herd led walking tours
